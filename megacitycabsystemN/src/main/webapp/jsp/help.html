Help & Support

Welcome to Mega City Cab Support! Here are some common questions and answers. If you need more help, feel free to contact us!

01. How do I book a ride?

Go to the Booking Page, fill in your details, and click "Book Now".

02. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us at +94 77 4568464 or email support@megacitycab.com. We are available 24/7.

03. How do I calculate the fare?

The fare is estimated based on distance. You can enter a rough estimate while booking.

04. What should I do if I lose an item in the cab?

Contact our support team immediately at +94 77 4568464. We will assist in recovering your lost item.

05. Can I cancel a ride after booking?

Yes, you can cancel a ride by calling customer support or using the cancellation option in the app (if applicable). Cancellation charges may apply.

06. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments via our mobile app.
